The Story

GearAgain is an ecommerce site where users can buy and sell used outdoor gear. I built the application using JavaScript, React, and Redux on the front end, and Express and Sequelize on the back end. I used Postgres for the database.

The idea for GearAgain came to me as I watched my wife buy and resell used clothing, shoes, and other items online. Our passion for the outdoors led me to build a used outdoor gear selling platform. When people use my app, we are all helping to preserve this beautiful planet on which we live and at the same time helping each other find quality outdoor gear for a great price.
